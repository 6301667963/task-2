My Favorite Books

To Kill a Mockingbird

Author: Harper Lee

Genre: Fiction

Description: A gripping, heart-wrenching, and wholly remarkable tale of coming-of-age in a South poisoned by virulent prejudice.

1984

Author: George Orwell

Genre: Dystopian, Political Fiction

Description: A dystopian social science fiction novel and cautionary tale about the future.

The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Genre: Tragedy

Description: A story of the mysteriously wealthy Jay Gatsby and his love for the beautiful Daisy Buchanan, set in the Jazz Age.

Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Genre: Romantic Fiction

Description: A classic novel that deals with issues of class, marriage, morality, and misunderstandings in the British Regency era.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Author: J.K. Rowling

Genre: Fantasy

Description: The first book in the Harry Potter series, following Harry as he discovers he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
